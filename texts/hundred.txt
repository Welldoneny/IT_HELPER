The first pale light of dawn crept through the curtains, signaling the start of a new day. Outside, the city was slowly stirring to life. A lone taxi hummed down the wet street, and a pigeon cooed softly on the windowsill. Inside, the quiet was absolute. The scent of fresh coffee began to drift from the kitchen, a comforting promise of warmth and energy. For a few precious moments, the world felt suspended between sleep and wakefulness. There were no urgent emails or rushing cars, just the gentle tick of the clock. This was the golden hour, a private slice of peace before the daily noise began.
